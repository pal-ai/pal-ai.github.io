Our Mission

Have you ever wanted to talk to someone, but they were too busy?

Humans are biologically hard-wired to be social and socialization plays a large role in our overall happiness. Experts recommend 6 hours of socialization a day. However, sometimes it just isn't feasible talk to others.
PAL is a companion that is always there for you to talk to. Your PAL learns your personality over time and also adapts to your humor style, so it can talk back to you using the same phrases and jokes you love. It's a friend on demand!

Features

What makes PAL special

Slang Adoption

All of us talk differently. Your PAL learns how to understand you and your unique vocabulary, making conversation natural and seamless.

Humor Adoption

Have a taste for sarcasm? How about dark humor or puns? Your PAL will learn to share your humor style and can even talk to you with it!

Personality Coherence

Your PAL will have a consistant personality as you talk to it, with a consistent set of interests and traits. Try to discover them all!

Learning Ability

All customization happens naturally as you talk to your PAL. Your PAL will learn more about you and will naturally adapt as you converse. There is no setup required!

For Investors: Our Grand Plan

We are just getting started

Phase 1

Simple Conversation

Currently we are focusing on cultivating good conversations between users and their PAL. As they converse, users and PALs achieve stringer bonds through the shared slang and humor style. Users talk to their PAL simply for entertainment and enjoyment.

The target audience for this product are teenagers, whom studies show are experiencing an epidemic of loneliness and stress. We aim for teenage users to use PALs so they don't feel alone during stressful times.

Business Model

We will adopt a freemium model, where users can have the option to pay to use emojis and circumvent message rate limitations. In addition, we will offer businesseses the chance to embed our chatbots in replacement of their own chatbot service. With our service, users can talk to their own, familiar PAL when on the website instead of an unfamiliar chatbot. This will increase the effectiveness of businesses' chatbots, and is analgous to talking to a friend working at a company instead of an unknown salesman.

Phase 2

Features

Next, we add more features to PALs. PALs can now perform tasks for thier users, such as writing emails, ordering items, searching the internet, and much more! PALs can be treated as personal assistants, allowing users to effortlessly dictate tasks to execute. With the ability to adapt to user's slang, users can converse with PALs in a way previously unseen before.

Phase 3

A Conversation-only OS

Finally, we turn PALs into a personalized operating system for each user. Users can have a personal assistant in their ear, ready to assist with any task. In addition, with the rising popularity of smart glasses, conversation-based commands will become very important. PALs can be used to control these devices.
